Chapter 1: Features of the Caste System, Pages 1-27

Two main features according to Baines:
1. Ubiquity: most general form of social organization
2. Strangeness: very different from social grouping in Europe or America

Features provided in the 3rd Century by Megasthenes:
1. Not permitted to contract marriage with a person of another caste
2. Not permitted to change from one profession or trade to another

After this, there were alterations in the system due to close cultural interaction with Western Europe
