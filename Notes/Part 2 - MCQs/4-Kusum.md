## SECTION L: TWO-MARK (LONGER) ANALYTICAL MCQs

**Q91 (2 marks).** Consider the following two customs: (i) Among the Mizos, if the husband initiates divorce he loses the bride-price paid, while if the wife initiates it she must return the bride-price.
