# Understanding Surrogacy

- Practice in which a woman (surrogate mother) bears a child for a couple unable to produce children in the usual way, usually because the wife is infertile.  
- Types:
	- Based on source:
		- Traditional Surrogacy - legal complications
		- Gestational Surrogacy
	- Based on compensation:
		- Altruistic
		- Commercial
- Anand Model
- National Guidelines for the Accreditation, Supervision and Regulation of ART clinics in India, 2005
- Beckert's Three Interwoven Domains of Value:
	- Physical Value: Tangible Benefits:
	- Positional Value: Social Status
	- Imaginative Value: Symbolic Associations
- The Surrogacy Regulation Act, 2021
